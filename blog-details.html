Best Rajasthani Places In Bangalore For 2022

  • Posted By : Piyush Ranbida
  • |
  • Time : 11.30 am

Rajasthani cuisine is one of the most loved cuisines in Bangalore thanks to its spiciness, serving of pickles, the assortment of delicious flavours on a thali and its celebration of culture on a plate. The Rajasthani gourmet preparations of Laal Maas, Dal Batti Churma, Gatte Ki Khichdi and the milk sweet Ghevar can make any foodie’s mouth water. So we have here our list of best Rajasthani places in Bangalore for you to feast on authentic Rajasthani food.

Rich Rajasthani Food At Kesariya, JP Nagar

When it comes to dining like Rajasthani royalty, Kesariya should be on your go-to list. The minute you walk into this place you will be spellbound by the haveli style decor of Shekhawati antique columns, walls adorned with traditional paintings, jharokhas and gorgeous silver crockery. You can feast on rich dishes like Kalmi Vada, Dal Bati Churma, Paneer Lal Mirch, Panchmele Ka Saag and much more. Finish your meal on a sweet note with the Jalebi Rasmalai Sandwich and refreshing Gulaab Sharbat.

Delicious Rajasthani Food At Rasovara, Lavelle Roadr

Located right in the heart of Bangalore’s favourite spot UB City, Rasovara serves you delicious Rajasthani dishes oozing with authentic flavours. You can start your meal with the special Rajdhani Chai, then proceed to have the medium spicy, crunchy Bhindi Rajasthani, delicious Paneer Maharani and Mirch Makai Pulao. And when it comes to desserts they have plenty to offer like Apple Jelabi, Kesari Malpua and Sizzling Halwa. This is one of the best Rajasthani places in Bangalore that you must visit.
